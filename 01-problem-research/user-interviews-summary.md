## The 2 Primary Personas

### Persona 1: Ramesh Kumar — "The Cash-Constrained Kirana Owner"
> *Represents M01, M03, M06, M09, M15, M18 — 6 of 20 merchants (30%)*

**Profile:**
- Age 38–45, runs 1–2 grocery/hardware/retail stores in Tier-2 cities
- Travels 2–4x/year for FMCG supplier meetings, wholesale market visits
- **No Paytm Business Credit** — pays for trips with personal savings or cash advance from business partner
- Low-to-moderate digital literacy; uses MMT/Goibibo mobile app but finds it confusing

**Key Quotes:**
> *"Booking takes 20 minutes, then I have to remember to save the receipt for my CA. Half the time I forget and lose the tax deduction."* — M01

> *"I use MakeMyTrip but I don't know how to get a GST bill from it. My accountant is always angry with me."* — M03

> *"I have to call my partner to transfer money before I can book. By the time that happens, the cheap seat is gone."* — M06

**Pain Hierarchy:**
1. 🔴 **Cash flow friction** — Must arrange advance cash before booking; can't book on the spot
2. 🔴 **GST invoice failure** — Manually creates or loses invoice; 40%+ error rate
3. 🟡 **Time waste** — 15–20 min per booking across 3+ tools
4. 🟡 **Post-trip filing** — Rarely files expenses correctly; loses tax deductions

**JTBD:** *Book a trip + get a compliant GST invoice + preserve working capital — all in under 5 minutes, from any device, without needing a separate cash arrangement.*

---

### Persona 2: Priya Shah — "The Credit-Enabled Boutique Traveler"
> *Represents M02, M04, M05, M07, M08, M10, M14, M17 — 8 of 20 merchants (40%)*

**Profile:**
- Age 28–40, runs a specialty boutique or wholesale trading business
- Travels 5–8x/year for sourcing, trade shows, supplier relationship management
- **Has Paytm Business Credit** (₹20K–₹80K approved limit) but rarely uses it for travel
- High digital literacy; uses WhatsApp Business daily; expects fast, clean digital experiences

**Key Quotes:**
> *"I book on Cleartrip, then manually type invoice details into Excel for my accountant. It's annoying and I make mistakes."* — M02

> *"I have a ₹50K credit limit on Paytm but I never thought of using it for flights. I always pay from my savings."* — M05

> *"When I'm packing inventory before a trip, I can't stop to open an app and tap through 6 screens. I wish I could just call someone."* — M08

**Pain Hierarchy:**
1. 🔴 **Idle credit line** — Has business credit but defaults to cash because OTAs don't surface it
2. 🔴 **Manual invoice reconciliation** — Books on one platform, manually re-enters data for accounting
3. 🟡 **Context-switching** — Manages bookings on OTA, payments via bank, invoices via Excel
4. 🟢 **Loyalty fragmentation** — Scattered across MMT, Cleartrip, Goibibo; no single business travel home

**JTBD:** *One-tap booking that auto-generates the correct GST invoice + uses my existing Paytm credit line — so I can book mid-inventory-packing and my CA gets the file automatically.*

## Consolidated Pain Points (All 20 Merchants)

| Pain Point | Current State | Frequency | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Time waste** | 17 min avg (OTA search → book → GST invoice → credit arrangement) | 20/20 | 30% abandon trips due to friction |
| **GST invoice errors** | 40% file incorrect invoices (wrong HSN code, missing GSTIN) | 18/20 | Lose tax deduction; CA rework hours |
| **No business credit on OTAs** | 60% use personal savings; 40% arrange cash advance | 17/20 | Cash flow strain; missed growth |
| **Tool fragmentation** | Use 3.2 tools on avg (MMT + Excel + bank app + WhatsApp) | 20/20 | Cognitive overhead; error-prone |
| **Post-trip expense filing** | 50% never file expenses properly | 14/20 | Ongoing money left on table |
| **Proactive trip planning** | React to trip needs instead of planning ahead | 16/20 | Last-minute = expensive bookings |

## Behavioral Findings

### How merchants currently book travel
- **MakeMyTrip mobile app:** 14/20
- **Goibibo:** 4/20
- **Cleartrip:** 5/20
- **Call a travel agent:** 3/20
- **Direct airline website:** 2/20

*(Multiple answers allowed)*

### How merchants currently handle GST invoices
- **Manually screenshot + edit in Paint/WhatsApp:** 8/20
- **Ask their CA to figure it out:** 6/20
- **Request invoice email from OTA (often fails):** 4/20
- **Don't claim GST at all:** 5/20
- **Use a proper GST tool:** 1/20

### Awareness of Paytm Business Credit for travel
- **"Never thought of using it for travel":** 11/20
- **"Tried once, too confusing on Paytm travel":** 3/20
- **"Actively uses credit for travel":** 2/20
- **"Doesn't have credit yet":** 4/20

## Hypotheses Validated

| Hypothesis | Status | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Merchants want GST invoice automation | ✅ Confirmed | 18/20 cited manual invoicing as top pain |
| Credit on OTAs is underused, not unwanted | ✅ Confirmed | 11/20 unaware of Paytm credit for travel |
| Voice is viable for this segment | ✅ Confirmed | 15/20 open to voice booking if "fast and right" |
| 3-min booking is achievable via voice | 🟡 To Validate | Pilot will measure actual call duration |
| WhatsApp is preferred delivery channel | ✅ Confirmed | 17/20 use WhatsApp Business daily |
